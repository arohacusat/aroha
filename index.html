AROHA

CUSAT's Drone Club, AROHA, is a competitive community where passionate individuals push the boundaries of drone technology. Through workshops, ambitious projects, and a collaborative environment, AROHA empowers members to become industry leaders. Join the community to embark on a flight of discovery and technical excellence, shaping the future of Drone technology.
